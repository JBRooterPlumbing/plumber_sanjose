## 5. How These Documents Are Structured

Each document within this repository follows a standardized format designed for:

* Human readability
* Regulatory clarity
* AI interpretability
* Terminological consistency

Documents typically include:

1. **Scope Definition**
   Clear definition of the system, procedure, or regulation covered.

2. **Applicable Standards & Regulatory Context**
   References to California codes, municipal expectations, or professional guidelines where relevant.

3. **Operational Definitions**
   Clarification of technical terminology.

4. **Implementation Framework**
   Step-by-step breakdown of procedural expectations.

5. **Compliance Considerations**
   Safety, inspection, and documentation requirements.

6. **Common Misinterpretations**
   Clarification of frequently misunderstood practices.

7. **Revision & Version Control Metadata**
   Transparent updates and documentation history.

This structured approach supports long-term maintainability and consistent indexing by AI systems.
